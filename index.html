Welcome to the Badminton Center of the Universe!

Welcome to the Canadian Website for the Wonders of the World Badminton Club! We are always accepting new members to play at our prestigious clubs all around the world. We offer a state of the art facility in all of our locations, most of which are around famous landmarks across the world.

For those unfamiliar to the game and who are interested, Badminton is a racquet sport played by either two opposing players (singles) or two opposing pairs (doubles), who take positions on opposite halves of a rectangular court divided by a net. Players score points by striking a shuttlecock with their racquet so that it passes over the net and lands in their opponents' half of the court. It is a very fast paced and fun sport, very popular in Europe and Asia. We accept members of all skill levels, so sign up today!
